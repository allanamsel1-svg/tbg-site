About TBG Sourcing Inc.

We're a technology company with roots in the consumer goods industry — we build the software we always needed.

The Company

TBG Sourcing Inc. is a technology company headquartered in Brooklyn, New York. We develop sourcing intelligence software for companies that source, manufacture, and distribute consumer goods — with a focus on the health, beauty, and personal care categories that define modern retail.

Our Story

TBG Sourcing was founded with a simple premise: the consumer goods industry deserved better software. For decades, sourcing teams have relied on spreadsheets, email threads, and disconnected systems to manage relationships with hundreds of factories, track thousands of SKUs, and coordinate the complex logistics of getting product from a factory floor in China to a retail shelf in the United States.

We knew there was a better way — because we had lived the problem firsthand. So we built Aletheia.

Our Platform

Today, TBG Sourcing develops and maintains Aletheia, a multi-tenant SaaS platform purpose-built for the consumer goods sourcing industry. Aletheia is used by importers, distributors, and brand owners who need a single system to manage their entire supply chain — from the first factory conversation to the final customer shipment.
