Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

TBG Sourcing Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects information you provide directly when you use our software platform, Aletheia, or when you contact us. This includes business contact information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, company names, and job titles. We also collect usage data, log data, and other information generated through your use of our services, including browser type, IP address, pages visited, and actions taken within the platform.

We do not collect sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, operate, and improve our software services; authenticate users and maintain account security; communicate with you about your account, product updates, and support requests; send operational notifications and transactional messages; analyze usage patterns to improve the platform; and comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your personal information for advertising or sell it to data brokers. We do not use your data to train third-party AI models without your explicit consent.

3. SMS Communications

TBG Sourcing Inc. may send SMS text messages to users who have provided their mobile number in connection with the Aletheia platform.

By providing your mobile number and opting in, you consent to receive transactional SMS notifications from TBG Sourcing Inc. regarding platform activity. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance. We do not sell or share your mobile number with third parties.

These messages include operational notifications such as order status updates, inspection alerts, shipment notifications, and account security alerts. Consent to receive SMS messages is not required as a condition of using our services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform (such as cloud hosting providers, payment processors, and communication services), subject to appropriate data processing agreements and confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of TBG Sourcing Inc., our users, or the public.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our platform uses industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. Access to personal data is restricted to personnel who need it to perform their job functions.

While we take security seriously, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and to contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it by law or for legitimate business purposes such as resolving disputes.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data; request a copy of your data in a portable format; object to or restrict certain processing; and withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
